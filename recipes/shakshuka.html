Shakshuka

Description

An easy shakshuka that makes a great brunch.
Serves 2

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 red onions, chopped
  • 1 garlic clove
  • Small batch coriander stalks
  • 2 cans cherry tomatoes
  • 4 eggs

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a frying pan that has a lid, then soften the onions, garlic, and coriander stalks for 5 mins until soft.
  2. Stir in the tomatoes, then bubble for 8-10 mins until thick.
  3. Make 4 dips in the sauce, then crack an egg into each one. Put a lid on the pan, then cook over a low heat for 6-8 mins, until the eggs are done to your liking.
  4. Serve with buttered crusty bread.
